Mouse Controls (Coordinate View)

ActionEffect
Scroll wheel upZoom in — range shrinks by factor 0.8, centred on the viewport
Scroll wheel downZoom out — range grows by factor 1.25
Left-button dragPan — drags the coordinate system in the direction of movement
Right-clickReset view — restores the range defined in Draw Options

To set a specific range, use Draw › Draw Options… and enter the desired X min / X max / Y min / Y max values. After pressing OK the view resets to that range.
